About Me

I am a developer and Brooklynite based in New Jersey, who loves making apps and indie games. During my sophomore year of college when I got bored of traditional writing, I discovered Twine, an Electron app by Chris Klimas for browser text games. I developed basic HTML/CSS skills while writing in Twine and then decided to learn actual programming languages, Javascript, Python and C#. Building digital homes, stores, and fictional worlds are my favorite things about programming.
