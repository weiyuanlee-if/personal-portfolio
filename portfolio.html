About Me

I am a New Jersey based designer who specializes in narrative design and web development. My first experience with development was in 2016, when I participated in My First Game Jam as a college student during winter break. I developed basic HTML/CSS skills while writing in Twine, a tool for narrative design where writers create interactive stories for browser play. I also learned C# and Javascript for web and game development. As a designer with a sociological background, I focus on diversity, storytelling, psychology, and disability. When I'm not programming or designing; I'm gaming, studying Mandarin or reading history books.
