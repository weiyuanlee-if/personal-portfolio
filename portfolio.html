About Me

I am a New Jersey based developer who specializes in UI/UX design, C# (Unity and .NET), and web development. My first experience with programming was in 2016, when I participated in My First Game Jam as a college student during winter break. I developed basic HTML/CSS skills while writing in Twine, a tool for narrative design where writers create interactive stories for browser play. I also learned C# for app and game development. As a developer with a sociological background, I focus on diversity, story telling, psychology, and disability. When I'm not programming or designing, I'm gaming, studying Mandarin or reading history books.
